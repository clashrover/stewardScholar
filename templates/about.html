Steward Scholar

Steward Scholar is a database website that maintains possible ethical issues with research papers.
Database is built on the assumption that an ethical issue raised for some paper will have causal effect on
linked research papers i.e the papers that cite the initial paper and so on.

Currently in development phase. The website provide following functionalities

  • Display Research Paper
    Search by ID. It will display information related to a research paper and flags associated with it
  • Add Research Paper
    Add a research paper to database
  • Add flag
    On display research paper page, user can add a flag, consequently all papers reachable from the current
    will get flagged with proper intesity.
  • Track Flag
    On display research paper page, user can click track flag button on some particular flag which will show the
    show one of the path from root research paper to current paper
