Steward Scholar

Steward Scholar is a database website that maintains possible ethical issues with research papers.
Database is built on the assumption that an ethical issue raised for some paper will have causal effect on
linked research papers i.e the papers that cite the initial paper and so on. Prime objective is to motivate researchers
to look for ethical issues that they have to be aware of arriving from their upcoming research
